Safe for all Children who just love to read

All books here are 100% age-appropriate and fun for you children to read.

Security is one of our top priorities. We value your children's safety. We check every story uploaded for content and images that may be harmful for any child. Additionally, parents can report books that they think will disturb their children for any books that may have passed our own security checks.

Our stories are user uploaded, but that is only the case to promote youth and young authors to write to their life desires. It is for encouragement and to create affordable material for children who need it.

Easy to use tracking and recording system

Parents can see the books their children are reading and watch their children's progress

Parents can look at the content their kids are looking at in their children's menu. They can track their search results and stories read. Parents even have the option of recommending books to their children.

Easy upload to make lives of authors simple

Using their favorite word processors, authors can just upload PDFs of their stories for children to read.

PDFs are easily transferrable and readable for any child. They allow more flexibility and duribility. Authors can make PDFs easily with their word processor. This makes it easy for them to share their stories to many kids around the world.
